Fashion Background

I am a self taught costume designer start sewing from 2017.Once Worked as David Tlale Voslorus to New York fashion as a dresser in 2019 Won Gauteng fashion glam showcase at the age of 20 in 2021 Made it to top 9 (as a stylist) at the Luckystar Reality Show competition on Mzansi magic in 2023

My design process starts from creating my graphic into the textile design of the graphic in the fabric There after look for inspiration that will help carry my vision out , then comes pattern making my technique involves geometric and my fabric adaption includes chemistry and relation . I SPEAK about the beauty of Africa and the geography about the world meanwhile I still empower people and offer the liberation one needs to free they minds
